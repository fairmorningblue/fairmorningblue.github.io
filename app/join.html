About Team Deloitte

Team Deloitte is more than 200 cyclists and volunteers dedicated to ending MS forever. Each year from our Houston and Austin offices, we work to raise funds, build community and spread awareness about MS. From our start together early on Saturday morning in Katy to the world’s best BBQ at our tent in La Grange, to our four-time-award-winning water stop, to the party we throw at the finish line, we are out to make an impact and to have a great time doing it. This year our goal is 150: 150 riders, $150,000, 150 miles.

About the BP MS 150

The BP MS 150, now in it’s 34th year, is the single largest fundraiser for Multiple Sclerosis in the world. 13,000 riders cover more than 150 miles between Houston and Austin, helped along by more than 3,500 volunteers and tens of thousands of contributors and spectators. This year’s ride takes place on April 28th and 29th and is set to bring in more than $14 million dollars for the National MS Society. Everyone rides the BP MS 150 for their own reasons, but we are united by one goal: a world free of MS. Find more information about the BP MS 150 at BPMS150.net.

How to Join

Sign up for the BP MS 150 - get outside, spend time with friends, improve your fitness, and become a part of the movement to end MS forever. Whether you’re one of those people that lives to ride or someone who hasn’t owned a bike since grade-school, we’ll help you get ready to make the journey and support you along the way. You can do this. To join, follow this link, select “Cyclist” and then “Join Team: Deloitte Bike Team”. Set your goal and you’re ready to roll!

Not up for riding but still want to be a part of Team Deloitte? We need your support! To make a donation to the MS Society on behalf of the team or on behalf of an individual rider, just go to our team page. We’re also looking for volunteers - everything from transportation to planning to food to the finish line - just let us know what you’re interested in and we’ll be in touch with details!
