Team Deloitte • Texas MS 150

Team Deloitte is made up of Deloitte people and our friends and family - cyclists, volunteers, and supporters committed to ending Multiple Sclerosis forever through the Texas MS 150.

Join the Team

Team Deloitte needs you! We’re looking to set a record for the largest Team Deloitte ever. With a new route and a new dedication to rider experience, this will be the best MS 150 to date. Learn more about joining the team as a cyclist, volunteer or supporter.

Rider Resources

Signed up to ride or still considering? Find a packing list, information on training rides, transportation information, jersey and kit ordering information and more.

Why We Ride

More than 2.3 million people across the world live with MS, facing symptoms that include blindness, paralysis, numbness, fatigue and pain. Learn more about the fight to end MS for good.
